Italy: Major Arms Exporter

Economy and Employment: The defense and armament sector represent a significant portion of the Italian economy, generating substantial revenue and supporting thousands of jobs.

Technology and Innovation: Investments in this sector also drive innovation and technological development. Technologies developed for the defense industry often find applications in other sectors as well, contributing to the overall technological progress of the country.

European Push in Arms Market

From the following article from “IlSole24Ore”, it can be read how the European Union is increasingly pushing for arms production, trying to keep up with Russia and the USA. In the text, in particular, it talks about:

  • Defense Investments: Ursula von der Leyen is pushing EU member states to increase investments in arms over the next five years.
  • European Security: The EU is seeking to strengthen its defense industry in response to security challenges, particularly following the war in Ukraine.
  • Arms Exports: The United States and Russia have dominated the global arms export market, but France is gaining ground following the decline in Russian exports.
  • European Production: Europe is facing challenges in ammunition and arms production, with Italy emerging as the leading arms producer on the continent.
